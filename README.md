I am a final-year CS student at **Brac University (Dhaka, Bangladesh)**, driven by a passion for creating **impactful and responsible AI systems** in the medical domain.

- **Thesis:** *Privacy-Preserving Hybrid Deep Learning Framework for Skin Disease Detection & Clinical Decision Support.*
- **Key Areas:** **U-Net + Transformers**, **Multimodal AI**, **Self-Supervised Learning**, and **Federated Learning**.
- **Mission:** Designing **Privacy, Explainability (XAI), and Real-World Applicability** into every AI model I build.

## 💡 Featured Projects & Contributions

High-impact projects demonstrating my expertise across AI, Deep Learning, and full-stack development.

- **🩺 Privacy-Preserving Skin Disease Detection (U-Net + Transformer)**
    > Combines **segmentation**, **multimodal learning**, and **federated privacy** for a robust, real-world clinical decision support system.
- **🧬 Diffusion-Based MRI Enhancement**
    > Used **Diffusion Models** (DDPMs) to denoise and reconstruct low-quality MRI scans, significantly improving image quality before segmentation.
- **💬 LLM-Integrated Clinical Decision Support**
